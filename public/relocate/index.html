Changing subteams is a big decision. Before proceeding, make sure you've given a lot of thought to the idea of changing your subteam, as you likely won't be able to change it again.

Changing your subteam is an agreement between you, the coach, the captain, the attendance manager, your current subteam lead, and your potential new subteam lead. To obtain the necessary approval, please send a single email addressed to the following people:

  • Coach: pwhite@jesuitmail.org
  • Captain: ahuang26@jesuitmail.org
  • Attendance Manager: apowvalla26@jesuitmail.org
  • Current Subteam Lead*
  • Desired Subteam Lead*
*Subteam lead emails listed at bottom

Make sure to include the following information in your email:

  • Your name
  • Your current subteam
  • Your desired subteam
  • Why you want to change subteams

Once you've sent the email, you'll receive a response from the coach as promptly as possible.

Other things to note:

  • Changing subteams is a big decision. Make sure you've given it a lot of thought.
  • Changing subteams is not guaranteed. The coach has the final say.
  • Changing subteams may not be possible if the desired subteam is full/overpopulated.
  • Changing subteams may not be possible at certain times during the year, especially midway through the season.
  • Changing subteams may only be possible once.

Once your request is approved, you'll want to meet with your new subteam lead at the next meeting to discuss how you'll move forward.
